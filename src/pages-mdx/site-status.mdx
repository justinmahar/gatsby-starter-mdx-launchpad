#### If the status indicates <Badge variant="success">Success</Badge>:
---

You are looking at the latest and greatest version of <CoreComponents.SiteName/>.

#### If the status indicates <Badge variant="warning">Building</Badge>:
---

A change was just made to <CoreComponents.SiteName/> and will be available to you shortly.

#### If the status indicates <Badge variant="danger">Failure</Badge>:
---

This usually means the owner of <CoreComponents.SiteName/> made a change that caused a problem (oops!). Nobody's perfect.

Until the problem is fixed, you may not see new updates to the site, but you can keep using the current version.
